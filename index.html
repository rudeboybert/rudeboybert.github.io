--- layout: default title: Albert Y. Kim - Middlebury College ---
Albert Y. Kim is an Assistant Professor of Statistics in the Mathematics Department at Middlebury College. Hailing from Montreal Quebec, he graduated from McGill University with a Joint Honours Mathematics and Computer Science B.Sc. in 2004. He then completed his PhD in statistics at the University of Washington in 2011 advised by Prof. Jon C. Wakefield. Subsequently, he was a data analyst in the AdWords division of Google Inc and a Visiting Assistant Professor of Statistics at Reed College.
